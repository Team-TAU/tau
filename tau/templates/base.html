About

The TAU app makes the real-time Twitch APIs easier to work with by unifying them under a single webhook connection. Gone are the days of setting up and tearing down your own webooks. No more struggling with token management. Simply point your bot at ws://localhost:PORT/ws/twitch-events/ and enjoy a live-stream of your follows, subs, cheers, and more!
